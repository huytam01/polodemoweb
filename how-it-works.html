How It Works

Our simple 4-step process from order to delivery

Step 1: Pick your style

Browse our polo shirts, t-shirts, and workwear. Each style comes with multiple fabric options — from budget-friendly poly-cotton to premium eco-friendly coffee fabric. Choose colors from our stock palette of up to 44 colors.

Step 2: Upload your logo

On the product page, drag and drop your logo file. We accept .AI, .EPS, .PNG (300dpi), and .PDF. Choose placement: left chest, right chest, back center, or sleeve. Don’t have a file ready? Email it to us after ordering.

Step 3: Pay and we create your mockup

Complete checkout with credit card, PayPal, or wire transfer ($2,000+). Within 24–48 hours, our design team creates a custom mockup showing exactly how your logo will look.

Important: We do NOT start production until you approve the design. You are fully in control. Up to 2 free revisions included.

Step 4: Approve and we produce & ship

Review your mockup via email or in your account dashboard. Click “Approve” and we start production. Timeline: 10–14 business days production + 5–7 days express shipping. Tracking provided at every step.

What if I’m not satisfied?

We offer a 100% quality guarantee. If the finished product doesn’t match the approved mockup, we replace it for free. We stand behind our work because we’re the manufacturer — not a middleman.
