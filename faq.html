Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions

Ordering

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
24 pieces for standard styles with your custom logo. For fully custom designs (custom fabric color, custom cut), the MOQ is 100 pieces.

Do I have to pay before seeing the design?
Yes, payment is collected at checkout. However, we create a detailed design mockup within 24–48 hours and you must approve it before we start production. Up to 2 free revisions are included. If you’re not satisfied at all, you can cancel for an 80% refund before production starts.

What logo file formats do you accept?
We accept .AI, .EPS, .SVG, .PDF (vector), and .PNG (300dpi minimum). Vector files (.AI, .EPS) produce the best embroidery results. Don’t have a vector file? We’ll recreate it for free with your first order.

Can I order different sizes in one order?
Absolutely. Our order form lets you specify exact quantities per size (XS through 4XL). Most customers order a mix of sizes for their team.

Can I mix different colors in one order?
Yes, as long as the total quantity meets the 24-piece minimum. For example: 12 navy + 12 white = 24 pieces total.

Pricing & Payment

Why are your prices so much lower than competitors?
We are the manufacturer — not a middleman. CustomInk and 4imprint buy from factories like ours and add 40–60% markup. You’re buying direct at factory price.

What payment methods do you accept?
Credit card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, and ACH bank transfer. For orders over $2,000, we also accept wire transfer with 30% deposit. Repeat customers (3+ orders) qualify for Net 30 payment terms.

Are there any hidden fees?
No. Our prices include the polo shirt + embroidery/print of your logo (1 location) + individual polybag packaging. No setup fees, no digitization fees, no surprise charges. Shipping is free for 24+ pieces.

Do you offer volume discounts?
Yes. Our pricing automatically adjusts based on quantity: 12–49, 50–99, 100–249, and 250+ tiers. See our bulk pricing page for details.

Production & Shipping

How long does production take?
10–14 business days after you approve the design mockup. Rush production (5–7 days) is available for +15%. Express shipping to the US takes 3–7 additional business days. Total: approximately 15–21 days from approval to delivery.

Do you ship internationally?
Yes. We ship to the US, Canada, EU, UK, and Australia. EU customers benefit from 0% import duty thanks to the EVFTA free trade agreement between the EU and Vietnam.

Can I track my order?
Yes. Once your order ships, you receive a DHL or FedEx tracking number via email. You can track your package in real-time.

What if I need my order urgently?
We offer rush production at +15% surcharge, which cuts production time from 10–14 days to 5–7 days. Combined with express shipping (3–5 days), you can receive your order in as little as 8–12 business days after approval.

Quality & Returns

What if the finished product doesn’t match the mockup?
We offer a 100% quality guarantee. If the product doesn’t match the approved design, we replace it for free. See our returns page for full details.

Can I get samples before ordering?
Yes! We offer 2 free sample polo shirts per company. Choose your preferred fabric and size, and we’ll mail them to your office. Request samples here.

How durable are your polo shirts?
Our polo shirts are designed for commercial use. The 65/35 poly-cotton piqué withstands 50+ industrial wash cycles without significant fading, pilling, or shrinkage. We provide AATCC test reports upon request.

What sizes do you offer?
XS through 4XL for all styles. We provide detailed size charts with measurements in both inches and centimeters on every product page.

About PoloViet

Where are your products manufactured?
In our own factory in Vietnam. Vietnam is the world’s 3rd largest garment exporter — Nike, Adidas, and Under Armour all manufacture here. Every shirt passes 5 quality control checkpoints.

Are you certified?
Yes. OEKO-TEX Standard 100 (safe materials), WRAP (ethical manufacturing), GRS (for recycled polyester), and we provide AATCC test reports (US wash test standards).

Can I visit your factory?
We welcome factory visits! Contact us to arrange a virtual tour via video call, or schedule an in-person visit if you’re traveling to Vietnam.
